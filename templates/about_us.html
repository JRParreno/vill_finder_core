About Us

VillFinder is a dedicated marketplace designed to assist aviation professionals in locating rental housing and establishments around Villamor. Our mission is to simplify the process of finding rental accommodations, making it easier for our clients to access the resources they need.

Our team consists of passionate 4th-year students committed to delivering an exceptional experience for our users:

  • Hans Stephen Arce: A key developer of the VillFinder app, Hans is focused on creating a seamless user experience.
  • Maica Lorie Mae Francisco: Responsible for documentation, Maica ensures that all aspects of VillFinder are well-organized and transparent.
  • Vin Matthew Vardeleon: As the project manager, Vin oversees the development process and ensures that our vision is realized effectively.

Together, we are dedicated to connecting aviation workers with the ideal rental options in their area. Welcome to VillFinder!

Rental Housing Philosophy

At VillFinder, we believe that locating rental housing near workplaces and schools enhances convenience for everyone involved. Our commitment is to simplify the rental process, creating a win-win situation for both renters and property owners.

Our digital platform offers a diverse array of rental options, ensuring that every user finds a solution that meets their needs. We strive to foster a collaborative experience that benefits the entire community, making it easier than ever to connect with the perfect home.
